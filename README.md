# UUIDKeys

[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)

**Enhancing the Trial Chambers. One Signature at a Time.**

**UUIDKeys** is a server-side Fabric mod built for players who love the vanilla Trial Chamber experience but want more reasons to team up, trade, and revisit their favorite chambers. 

---

## From Vanilla to UUIDKeys

If you've played Minecraft 1.21, you already know the basics:
- **Vanilla Rule 1**: Defeating a Trial Spawner ejects rewards for every player who participated.
- **Vanilla Rule 2**: Each player can open a Vault exactly **once** to get their loot.

**UUIDKeys takes these rules and adds a layer of social strategy.** It replaces the vanilla "one-reward-per-player" limit with a **Signature System**. Instead of just checking who *you* are, the Vault now checks who your **team** is.

### The Team Signature
Every Trial Key and Ominous Trial Key dropped from a spawner is automatically "signed" by the group that defeated it. Because the Vault treats setiap unique team combination as a fresh reward slot, **you can loot the same Vault multiple times** as long as you have a new combination of friends!

---

## Strategic Signature Management

Once you have your signed keys, you can use the crafting table to master the "Social Economy" of the Trial Chambers.

### Splitting: Fairness for Groups
In vanilla, playing with a large group is fun, but you might feel like you're missing out on the "Solo" experience. 
- **The Quirk**: Take a "Group Key" (signed by 4 people) and **Split** it in a crafting table to slice off your personal name.
- **The Benefit**: This creates a new **Solo Pass**. Since the Vault sees `[A]` as a different signature from `[A,B,C,D]`, you can use your team victory to "sweep" back through for those solo reward slots you haven't claimed yet.

### Merging & Trading: The Social Economy
Met a new player in the world? Use **Trading and Merging** to turn a handshake into a payday.
- **The Quirk**: Trade your solo keys and **Merge** them with a new friend's keys in a crafting table.
- **The Benefit**: Even if you didn't fight the same spawner, merging creates a brand new team signature. This unlocks a shared reward slot for both of you at every Vault, incentivizing you to cooperate with everyone you meet!
- **The Limit**: You can only merge keys up to the "Victory Headcount" (the number of original fighters). Large-group victories are precious, as they are the only way to craft high-capacity "Party Passes."

---

## Why Cooperate?

- **Re-Loot Any Vault**: Every new friend you team up with is a ticket to re-loot every Vault in your world.
- **Efficient Progression**: Use Splitting to get full "Reward Coverage" (Solo through Quad) from a single large battle instead of grinding individual keys.
- **Vanilla Friendly**: Using a signed UUIDKey is a parallel bonus system. Your standard vanilla loot is always protected and available.
