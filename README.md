# UUIDKeys

[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)

**Expand Your Rewards. Build Your Team. Conquer the Vault.**

**UUIDKeys** is a server-side Fabric mod that adds an exponential reward system to Trial Chambers. By introducing "Combinatorial Signatures," it gives players reason to form new teams, trade keys, and revisit Vaults for a shared prize.

---

In vanilla Minecraft, a Vault is a one-time reward for each player. UUIDKeys builds on this by recognizing **who your team was** during the battle.

Instead of just checking if *you* have looted a Vault, the Vault now checks for your **Team Signature**. 
- A key from a solo fight has 1 signatures.
- A key from a duo fight has 2 signatures.
- Etc.

Because the Vault treats every unique combination of players as a fresh reward slot, you can return to the same Vault multiple times. Even if you have already opened it alone, arriving with a new friend unlocks the Vault all over again for the group.

---

## Signature Management

### 1. Splitting
Playing in a large group is the most efficient way to get keys, but splitting is how you make them go further.
- Place a "Group Key" in a crafting table. By "pulling" your name off the list, you receive a **Personal Key** for yourself, while the **original key** (now minus your name) remains in the crafting grid for your friends.
- **Splitting is the primary way to get more physical keys.** If your group wins together, you can have every member split their name off to create a mountain of personal keys. This allows you to "sweep" back through the same Vault for every possible reward combination.

### 2. Merging
**The Team Limit**: Every Trial Key "remembers" how many players were there for the original fight. You can only merge keys if the total number of names stays within this original limit.
- **You can only merge keys that have been split (Personal Keys).** If a key is already full of names, you must split it to "make room" for a new friend's signature.
- Combine your personal keys with your friends' personal keys in a crafting table to create a brand new **Team Signature**.
- This allows you to strategically build signatures you need to unlock every reward slot that your original group victory allows.

### 3. Trading
Signatures are **not exclusive**. If you hold a key with a specific signature, you can use it even if you weren't part of the original fight—and so can everyone else you trade with!
- Trade your signed keys with others on the server.
- If multiple players have copies of the same signature, **every single one of them** can use it to get rewards from the same Vault. 

---

## Why Cooperate?

- **Infinite Re-Looting**: Every new friend you team up with is a ticket to re-loot every Vault in your world.
- Use Splitting to claim solo and team rewards from a single large battle instead of grinding individual spawners.
- Trading and sharing signatures allows your entire community to unlock the full potential of every Trial Chamber.
